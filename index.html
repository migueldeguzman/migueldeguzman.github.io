Projects

Archetypal Transfer Learning (ATL)

Archetypal Transfer Learning (ATL) is an innovative research method I developed to harness the power of meta-patterns or 'archetypes' in Large Language Models (LLMs). By utilizing concepts like hyper-reinforcement and distribution matching, ATL seeks to improve AI alignment, ensuring that these models operate safely and ethically. While the dual-use nature of ATL-P1 results necessitates caution in public dissemination, interested parties can access the findings through a dedicated Google Form.
